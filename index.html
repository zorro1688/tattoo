AI Tattoo Generator FAQ

Is this a real tattoo design or just AI art?

InkFirst creates references for tattoo planning. A professional tattoo artist should still refine the final design for your skin, size, and placement.

Can I bring the design to a tattoo artist?

Yes. The artist brief and linework view are meant to make your studio conversation clearer.

Can I use this AI tattoo generator for my first tattoo?

Yes. The product is designed around first tattoo decisions like idea choice, placement, size, and complexity.

What is linework or stencil export?

Linework is a cleaner outline-style reference that is easier to discuss than a full AI concept image.

Can I preview placement?

Yes. The placement preview helps you understand how body location affects scale and composition.
