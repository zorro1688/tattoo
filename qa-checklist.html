InkFirst Full Flow QA

Run this checklist before shipping changes to login, image generation, Creem payment, downloads, Billing, or Supabase Storage.

Pre-flight

  • Run npm run check:full-flow.
  • Confirm ngrok and NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL match.
  • Confirm Creem webhook points to the current public URL.

Google login

  • Sign in with Google.
  • Confirm the top-right control becomes Account.
  • Confirm Account menu shows the email.

Generate and save

  • Generate a recognizable concept.
  • Confirm Hero updates to the new image.
  • Confirm the design appears in My Designs.

Design detail

  • Concept shows the generated image.
  • Linework shows an empty state until generated.
  • Placement uses the current design.

Payment and download

  • Before purchase, downloads are watermarked.
  • Complete Creem checkout.
  • After purchase, available files say Download high-res.

Billing verification

  • Billing shows account email.
  • Plan and paid credits are correct.
  • Payment history shows provider, credits, and event ID.
